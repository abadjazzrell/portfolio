Who I Am - background & current

My name is Jazzrell Frian P. Abad, born on February 17, 1991, I am a 32-year-old computer enthusiast with a passion for programming and software engineering. Although I was unable to finish my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science due to financial reasons, I have since gained a solid foundation in the field through my studies and experience.

Before venturing into the tech industry, I started my own computer rental shop, which I successfully ran for six years. I then gained experience in the BPO sector where I worked for three years. However, my love for programming never faded and I pursued it further by enrolling in a coding bootcamp at Zuitt.

In the bootcamp, I learned several programming languages including HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Tailwind, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and React, along with knowledge in JavaScript.

Since completing the bootcamp, I have been actively learning more about programming and have a positive attitude towards being a part of a team in the IT industry. In my free time, I enjoy playing computer games and browsing the internet to learn new things.

I am driven, passionate, and eager to make a mark in the tech industry. With my skills, experience, and positive attitude, I am sure to be a valuable addition to any team.
